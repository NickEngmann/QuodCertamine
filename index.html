WHO IS NICK ENGMANN?

Throughout his college career Nick Engmann developed a thirst for knowledge. He majored in Electrical Engineering but he found himself taking classes in advertising, history, software developing, machine learning and eventually picked up a government minor as well. Upon graduating from the University of Texas at Austin Nick Engmann wanted to continue to fill his desire for learning by founding Quod Certamine.

Nick Engmann envisioned Quod Certamine as a job opportunity for individuals who wanted to use their diverse skillsets to create new innovations. Nick Engmann also saw Quod Certamine as a learning opportunity for new graduates who also want to test their minds.

Quod Certamine uniquely replicates some aspects of the college experience. Quod Certamine fosters an environment where employees can study and learn from each other and the resources that Quod Certamine has at its fingerpits to help engineers and designers build bigger and better products for their clients.
